## 1. THE DEVELOPMENT PARADIGM: VIBE CODING
The birth of Interimate represents a milestone in modern software engineering. The platform was built using the **"Vibe Coding"** philosophy in direct collaboration with **Antigravity**, an advanced agentic AI. 

### Core Development Philosophy:

### Advanced Pre-prompting Engineering:
A critical component of the platform’s intelligence is the **Advanced Pre-prompting Layer**. We have engineered a series of high-context "System Directives" that govern the AI’s behavior:
- **Tone & Persona Enforcement**: The AI is pre-prompted to act as a rigorous technical interviewer, utilizing the "Sigma" persona—straightforward, technical, and zero-bluff.
- **Strict Logic Constraints**: Pre-prompts include negative constraints that forbid the AI from providing answers in the initial templates, ensuring a "Zero-Leak" environment.

- **Contextual Anchoring**: The AI is anchored to specific curricula (JDBC, XPath Axes, SQL DDL/DML) before every generation call to maintain thematic consistency.

## 4. MAJOR ENGINEERING CONCEPTS

### A. Deduplication Core 2.0
To ensure every training session is fresh, the platform utilizes a deep-sampling deduplication engine. Before synthesizing a new challenge, the AI reviews a 15+ item history of previous questions, guaranteeing a 0% repetition rate and maximum conceptual variety.

### B. Surgical Code Review
Interimate does not rely on simple string matching. It uses the AI Core to perform a technical review of user-submitted code, understanding the logic and providing a precise **"FIX: "** snippet only when necessary.

### C. Sigma Notify Interface
Replacing native browser alerts, this custom-built notification engine provides high-tech, glassmorphism-styled popups that slide into the screen with status-based neon accents (Success/Warning/Error).
